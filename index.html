Overall Score

Your wellness score from 0 to 100. It shows how well you're maintaining good posture throughout your session. Higher is better!

Good Posture %

The percentage of time you've been sitting with your weight evenly distributed and your back straight.

Stability

How consistent your sitting position is. Higher values mean you're sitting still and balanced, with fewer sudden movements.

Weight Distribution Map

Shows where your weight is concentrated on the chair. Each square lights up based on pressure: front, back, left, or right. Balanced posture lights up all four evenly.

Your Timeline

Each small square is one moment in your session. Purple = good posture, yellow = leaning, dark = away from chair. Click any square to see the exact time and what you were doing.

Session Quality

A quick breakdown of your session: how much time sitting well, leaning, or away from your chair.
